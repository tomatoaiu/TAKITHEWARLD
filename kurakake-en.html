Kurakake Mountain(鞍掛山)

Kurakake Mountain is aiming at the southern foot of Iwate Mountain, and the altitude is 897 meters. Climbing mountainers are also relatively easy to climb and the familiarity that you can enjoy from the fresh green season to snow haiku is also popular, with more than 30,000 climbers visiting each year.

In recent years there is also the influence of the mountaineering boom, and it is showcasing with trekking around Ainosawa. In addition, the scenery changes according to the season Kurakake Mountain has many repeaters, and the more people climb every day, the more it is familiar to mountaineering enthusiasts.

About the climbing course

The climbing route starts from the Ainosawa campground and you can enjoy two courses. It is a hiking course that makes it easy to enjoy both courses.

In the middle of the course, a viewpoint that overlooks the vast Koiwai farm is waiting.

Duration

  • Mountain peak from Ainosawa campground (about 1 hour 20 minutes)
  • From the top of the mountain to the Ainosawa campground (about 1 hour)

Kurakake Mountain Foothills Promenade

At the foot of Kurakake Mountain there are two walking paths, Ainosawa Makino viewing course and Forest inside course.
