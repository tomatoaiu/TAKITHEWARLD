Mt.Iwate(岩手山)


"Southern piece Fuji", Iwate Prefecture's highest peak, which is known under the name of, at an altitude of 2,038 meters Shuho "Iwate Mountain".
A mountain of the Konide type that spreads a large field in the southwest part of Hachimantai National Park, northwest of Takizawa city. Volcanic terrain by Since the dawn of history 5 times of eruption is rare in Japan, from the Takizawa city, it is possible to admire the sight of beautiful Iwate Mountain show the four seasons rich facial expressions from anywhere, has been loved as a mountain of hometown. Many climbers visit from all over the country during the summer mountain season starting from July 1 every year and aim for the summit. In the mountain where the altitude is 2,000 meters above the mountain peak there is a rare "Comersa" group climate, plenty of beautiful alpine plants greet mountaineers. The view from the top of the mountain can see Mt. Iwaki and Mt. Hakkoda, far away from Chokaiyama and enjoy 360 degree panorama.

About the climbing course

Course "Yanagisawa Course" with horseshoe of Takizawa City (altitude 633 meters) as the climbing mouth is about 5.7 kilometers to the summit. And a new road of forest course, each of the old road of Galle field course with the feature, is a typical course of Iwate Mountain. There is a branch on the 2.5 th turn, the old road towards the top is the old road, the right is the new road. Both of them can fully enjoy Iwate Mountain climbing. The recommendation is to go on the new road till the 4th consecutive election route, and then proceed to the old road from there. If the weather is good you will be greeted by the view overlooking Lake Obihiro. You can also see the pretty alpine plants such as Miyakamai Bamai and Musashi Hiroshima if you turn your eyes on your feet. Joining with the new road at the seventh meeting, if you proceed for a while you will see the 8th evacue hut on your right. I aim for the summit moisturizing my throat with delicious fresh water in front of the hut. It will reach the mountaintop of 2,038 meters in about an hour from the 8th gear. Shimoyama goes through the new road of the forest course from the seventh consecutive day. Occasionally you can enjoy the landscape spreading in the ophthalmology.
